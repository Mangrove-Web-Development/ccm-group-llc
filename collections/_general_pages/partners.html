BedRock Real Estate Partners

BedRock Real Estate Partners, based in New York, is a privately held investment manager specializing in the development and acquisition of urban rental apartments and mixed-use projects in Boston, Washington D.C. and New York City.

 

 

Chartwell Property Group

Chartwell Property Group, based in Raleigh, North Carolina, works closely with owners, investors, lenders and corporate users of real estate to develop and execute sophisticated real estate strategies aimed at improving operations, unlocking value, or minimizing risk.

 

Collins Enterprises

Collins Enterprises, based in Old Greenwich, Connecticut, with offices in Norfolk, Virginia, creates value and profitability through strategic redevelopment and repositioning of underused properties. The firm is nationally recognized for its work in urban waterfronts, reemerging downtowns and brownfield sites. 

 

 

Interlink

Interlink, based in San Juan, Puerto Rico, is an integrated real estate resource offering clients and partners unmatched expertise in development, construction, and asset and property management.

 

 

Laurel Street

Laurel Street, based in Charlotte, North Carolina, is a private development company that plans and develops high-quality mixed-income communities throughout the East Coast for working families and seniors that are affordable to households of various incomes.

 

 

Middleburg Communities

Middleburg Communities, based in Vienna, Virginia with offices in North and South Carolina, is a fully integrated multifamily development, investment, construction and management company operating in the southeastern and mid-Atlantic United States. The firm’s success is rooted in a genuine desire to serve its local communities in thoughtful and holistic ways.
