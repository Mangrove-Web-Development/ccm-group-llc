CASTO Southeast

In a collaboration with CCM, CASTO expanded its investments into the U.S. Southeast region, focusing on Florida and North Carolina markets. The partnership crystallized and operates today as Casto Southeast. Its portfolio consists of a wide range of products managed and leased, including neighborhood grocery-anchored centers, mixed-use projects and triple-net outparcel developments. CASTO Southeast also has a robust third-party management and leasing presence overseeing 4.6 million square feet.

CASTO and CCM Group

In 1999 the partnership of CASTO Southeast and CCM Group completed a redevelopment of the former Winter Park Mall in Winter Park, Florida, into an open-air lifestyle center and renamed the center Winter Park Village, which was the first of its kind. The 524,000-square-foot mixed-use project features more than 350,000 square feet of upscale retail and entertainment, 115,000-square-feet of office and 58 loft apartment units.

This redevelopment propelled the partnership into the mixed-use development arena. Within a decade of entering the mixed-use market, our partnership built Park West Village, a 100-acre, 504,000-square-feet mixed-use project in Morrisville, North Carolina, among other projects in the southeast United States.

Today, CSE-CCM Real Estate Investments is the holding company for Winter Park Village, Park West Village, and other properties in operation and under development.
