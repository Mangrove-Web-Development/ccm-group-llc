CCM Group is a real estate investment firm based in Washington, DC, focusing on long-term strategic partnerships that deliver superior, risk-adjusted returns.

CCM’s purpose-driven values, diverse portfolio, expertise, and longevity make it a sought-after investor for emerging and established commercial developers seeking flexible capitalization structures.

- _id: general_content general_content_html: >-

Poised for growth

CCM’s holdings, either outright or in partnerships, span 8 million square feet in over 100 single-purpose entities within the eastern United States and Puerto Rico. We are well-positioned for accelerated growth with diverse property investments in multiple geographies producing stable cash flows and selective recycling of capital.

We value deep relationships

Our team’s work ethic, professionalism, and exceptional relationships with our partners reflect CCM’s integrity and collaborative approach. Our employees have deep company ties, span family generations, and many have achieved decades of continuous service honing deep expertise.

The purpose behind our motivation

We are equally proud of the superior return on investment we deliver to our partners and the impact that enables us to make through our charitable endeavors at the Flamboyan Foundation, which equips children affected by inequality to succeed in school and beyond.
